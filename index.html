AltusFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02/06/2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AltusFlow ("we", "us", "our" or "app") collects, uses, shares and protects your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized service of creating intelligent routines, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register via Email, Google, or Facebook, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture (if available).
  • Usage and Content Information: We collect the data you enter into the app, such as your goals, tasks, free time, and the routines generated by Artificial Intelligence.
  • Device Information: We collect basic data about the device you are using (model, operating system, language, and time zone) to ensure that notifications and translations work correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected to:

  • Generate personalized productivity routines through Artificial Intelligence.
  • Sync your progress, streak history, and routines saved in the cloud for access across multiple devices.
  • Send local notifications (daily reminders) in the correct time zone.
  • Improve and optimize the operation of the application.

3. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. However, AltusFlow uses third-party services that are essential for the operation of the app, which may process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Cloud Services and Authentication: Firebase (Google) for database and login.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Services such as Google Gemini, Groq, and OpenRouter (depending on the AI model you choose) to process the text and generate the routines. AIs process requests, but they don't tie prompts to your personal identity.
  • Payment Processing: RevenueCat, Google Play Billing, and Apple App Store manage Premium plan subscriptions. We do not have access to your credit card details.
  • Advertising: Google AdMob (for free plan users) may use device information to show you relevant ads.

4. Data Deletion and Control

You have complete control over your data. At any time, you can delete individual routines or permanently delete your account directly via the "delete account" button in the app's Profile, or use our external link: Account Deletion Request. When you delete your account, all of your personal data and history will be removed from our servers.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email: altusflowstudios@gmail.com or use our in-app support form.
